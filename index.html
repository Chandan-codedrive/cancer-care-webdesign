Welcome Our Foundation

Tara Cancer Foundation is an organization founded in the memory of Late Tara Ben Patel. The foundation aims to help in the fight against cancer. The objectives of the foundation are to spread awareness about cancer-related facts and treatments and provide necessary help to underprivileged cancer patients. The organization also aims to provide the best accessibility for palliative care to advanced cancer patients.
